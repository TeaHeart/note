# 连词

---

- 连词(conj.)是一种虚词, 它**不能**独立担任句子成分而只起连接词与词, 短语与短语以及句与句的作用

## 一.分类

- 含义分
- 并列连词, 转折连词, 选择连词和因果连词

- 用法分
- 并列连词, 丛属连词

## 二.含义分

1. 以 and 为代表表示意义引申的连词
   - both...and(都), as well as(也), not only (but) also(不仅... 而且), not ...nor(既不... 也不)
   - then(然后), neither...nor(都不)
   - He can't speak French, nor can he read French.
   - They sat down and talked about something.
   - She plays not only the paino, but (also) the guitar.
2. 以 or 为代表代表选择
   - whether...or, either...or
   - I must work hard, or I'll fail in the exam.
   - Either you or I am right.
3. 以 but 为代表表示转折或对比
   - while(尽管), whereas(尽管), however(然而), only(仅仅)
   - Some people love cats, while others hate them.
   - They were not the bones of an animal, but (the bones) of a human being.
4. 只有 for(因为)一个连词表示原因(句中)
   - He is absent today, for he is ill.

## 三.用法分

1. 并列连词
   - 一个简单句中, 只能由一个谓语动词
   - A. The car **stopped**.
   - B. The driver **got** out.
   - The car stopped, the driver got out.(错误)
   - 使用连词进行连接
   - The car stopped **and** the driver got out.
2. 丛属连词
   - 主要用于引导复合句
   - 简单从属连词
   - although(尽管), because(因为), before(在... 之前), if(如果)
   - once(一旦), since(既然), till(直至), unless(除非)
   - until(直到), where(), whether(无论)
   - Although there are some problems caused by SARS, government already provides solutions.
   - Don't ask me to explain unless you really don't understand.
   - 复合从属连词
   - as if(好像), as far as(就... 而言), as soon as(一... 就...), according to(按照)
   - in case(一旦), now(that)(既然), on cndition that(在... 条件下), no matter who(how/what/when/where)(
   - 无论谁/怎样/什么/何时/何地)
   - in order that(为了)
   - Fields of corn spread out as far as the eye could see.
   - The days are closing in now that it is September.
   - She made a grand toilet as of for a ball.
   - 关联从属连词
   - as...as(与... 一样), so...that(如此... 以致), no sooner...that(不等... 就), such as(比如)
   - whether...or(无论是否), so...as(太... 以致)
   - We can go in my car, such as it is.
   - We must stick to our promise whether or no.
