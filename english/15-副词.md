# 副词

---

- 副词(adv.)是一种用来修饰动词, 形容词, 全句的词, 说明时间, 地点, 程度, 方式等概念的词, 副词是一种半虚半实的词, 在中文中如...
  地

## 一.分类

1. 时间副词
   - now, then, often, always
   - usually, today, next, already(已经)
   - generally(一般地), frequently(频繁), seldom(很少的), ever(曾)
   - never, yet, immediately(立即), finally
   - shortly(很快), before, ago(之前), sometimes, once, twice
2. 地点副词
   - here, there, everywhere, anywhere
   - somewhere, forward(向前的)
3. 方式副词
   - carefully, properly(适当地), anxiously(焦虑地), suddenly
   - normally(正常地), calmly(冷静地).politely(有礼貌地), proudly(自豪地)
   - softly(轻柔地), warmly(温暖地), solwly(缓慢地)
4. 程度副词
   - much, little, very, rather(相当)
   - so, too, still, quite
   - perfectl(完美地), enough, extremly(非常), entirely(整个)
   - almost, slightly(细小地), hardly
5. 疑问副词
   - how, when, where, why
6. 关系副词
   - when, where, why
7. 连接副词
   - therefore(因此), moreover(此外), however, otherwise(另外的)
   - then, when, where, how
   - why

## 二.形容词变副词

1. 大多数情况下, adv. = adj. + ly
   - quick - quickly, bad - badly, sudden - suddenly
   - 例外 friendly 不是副词
2. 以辅音字母+y 结尾的，变 y 为 i 再加 ly
   - heavy - heavily

## 三.比较级和最高级

- 和形容词一样

1. 单音节词直接在后面加 er/est
   - hard - harder - hardest
2. 以 e 结尾的，加 r/st
   - wide - wider - widest
3. 辅音字母+y 结尾的，变 y 为 i 再加 er/est
   - early - earlier - earliest
4. 部分双音节和大部分多音节的词，在前面加 more 和 most
   - slowly - more slowly - most slowly
   - carefully - more carefully - most carefully
5. 不规则变化
   - well - better - best
   - much - more - most
   - little - less - lease
   - far - father - farthest
   - badly - worse - worst

## 四.副词的位置

- 时间副词和地点副词的位置一般放在句尾, 同时出现时, 把地点副词放在时间副词前面, 或把时间副词放在句首
- They went boating in Zhongshan Park yesterday.
- Yesterday they went boating in Zhongshan Park.
- 表示不确定的时间(频率副词)副词通常放在行为动词之前, 情态动词, 助动词和 be 动词之后
- We should always work hard.
- We are never late.
- He often comes late.
- 程度副词一般放在被修饰词之前(enough 例外)
- He is very careful.
- enough + 名词
- Is there enough milk in your coffee?
- enough 不和名词使用
- You are old enough to do this
- 形容词 + enough
- It isn't warm enough here.
- too + 形容词/副词
- too much/too many = more than you want, more than is good
- I don't like the weather here. There is to much rain.
- Let's go to another restaurant. There are to many people.
- to + adj.
- The hat is too big for him.
- not + adj. + enough
- The hard is no big enough for him.
- 多数方式副词都可以放在动词之后, 如果动词带有宾语, 就放在宾语后面
- He works hard.
- 修饰名词时, 一般放在名词之后, 修饰介词时, 放在介词之前, 修饰全句时, 放在句首
- The person there is looking for you.
- There's the house, right in front of you.
- Truly he will go to Shanghai.
