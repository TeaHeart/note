# 介词

---

- 介词是一种用来表示词与词, 词与句之间的关系的虚词, 在句中**不能**单独作句子成分
- 介词和它的宾语构成介词词组, 在句中作状语, 表语, 补语, 或介词宾语, 介词可以分为时间介词, 地点介词, 方式介词, 原因介词和其他介词
- 介词和其他代词或名词搭配形成介词短语(常见考点)

## 一.时间介词

1. in/on/at + 时间 在... 时候
   - at + 时间点
   - at 7:30 / at seven o'clock
   - quarter, half
   - on + 日子
   - on May 1st
   - January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December
   - on Monday
   - Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
   - in + 时间段
   - 某世纪/年/季/月
   - in 21st century
   - in 2022
   - at night/midnight
   - at noon
   - at the weekend
   - at the end of...
   - at the moment
   - Are you going away at the weekend?
   - I'm going on holiday at the end of October.
   - 就近原则
   - on Monday morning / on Tuesday afternoon / on Friday evening / on Saturday night
   - I'm meeting Joanne on Monday morning.
   - Are you doing anything on Saturday night?
   - this/last/next/every, 不能和 at/on/in 一起使用
   - Are you going out this evening?
   - We go on holiday every summer. Last summer we went to Canada.
2. from...to/until/since/for
   - from...to... 从... 到...
   - We lived in Japan from 1992 to 2001.
   - I work from Monday to Friday.
   - until 直到
   - They'll be away until Friday.
   - I read a book until 3 o'clock.
   - since + 时间点 自从
   - We use since after the present perfect (have been / have done).
   - Joe is in hospital. He has been in hospital since Monday.
   - for + 时间段 用于现在完成时
   - Joe has been in hospital for three days.
3. before/after/during
   - before 在... 之前
   - Everybody feels nervous before exams.
   - after 在... 之后
   - I fell asleep during the exam.
   - during 在... 期间
   - My mom brings me to the park after the exam.

## 二.地点/方位介词

1. in/at/on
   - in/on
   - in 在... 里面
   - Rachel works in a shop.
   - I went for a swim in the river.
   - on 在... 表面
   - Don't sit on the grass. Is't wet.
   - There is a stamp on the envelope.
   - 特殊
   - in/on tree
   - There are some birds in the tree. 原本不属于树
   - There are some apples on the tree. 原本属于树
   - in/on sofa/bed
   - in 强调陷入 人
   - on 强调表面 物
   - You must stay in bed for another two days.
   - A doll is on the bed.
   - in/at
   - in + 大地方
   - on + 小地方
   - to/in/at
   - go/come/return/walk to... 去/来/回去/走... 地方
   - We're going to London on Sunday.
   - I want go to Ltaly next year.
   - be/staydo something in... 在... 地方 做... 大地方
   - My brother lives in Ltaly.
   - I like reading in bed.
   - be/stay/do something at... 在... 地方 做... 小地方
   - The bus is at the airport.
   - I met a lot of people at the patry.
   - home adv.
   - go/come/walk home (without to)
   - be/stay/do something at home.
   - I'm tired. I'm going home.
   - I'm staying at home tonight.
2. under(正下方)/behind/opposite(对面)
   - next to / beside(旁边) / between / in front of(前面) / behind
   - on the left/right
   - in the middle.
   - by(=next to / beside)
   - Out house is by/beside the sae.
   - under
   - The cat is under the table.
3. above(在...上面)/below（在...下面）
   - The pictures are above the shelves.
   - The shelves are below the pictures.
4. up/over/through
   - to + 目标
   - from + 源
   - Jane is going to France next week.
   - A lot of English words come from Latin.
   - in/into 进去的过程中
   - out of 出来的过程中
   - We jumped into the water.
   - I took the old batteries out of the radio.
   - on 放在... 上
   - off 拿下/落下
   - Don't put your feet on the table.
   - Don't fall off your bicycle.
   - up 上升
   - down 下降
   - We walked up the hill to the house.
   - Be careful! Don't fall down the stairs.
   - over 上面过去
   - under 下面过去
   - The pland flew over the mountains.
   - Some people say it is unlucky to walk under a ladder.
   - through 穿过
   - round/around 环绕
   - A bird flew into the room through a window.
   - The new road gose round the village.
   - along 沿着...
   - across 一边到另一边
   - I was walking along the road with my dog.
   - Let's go for a walk along the river.

## 三.常见搭配

1. 形容词 + 介词
   - afraid of, full of, angry with, strict with
   - busy with, good at, good/bad for, late for
   - sorry for, ready for, famous for, polite to
   - far from ...
2. 动词 + 介词
   - listen to, laugh at, write to, hear from
   - get to, look at, shout at, shout to
   - knock at, look for, look at, ask for
   - wait for, get on, get to, put on
   - turn on, operate on, take off, turn off
   - learn from, worry about ...
